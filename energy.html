*** ⚡: Power report rdy
We run connected to the grid. This is inherently a mix of different resources: Gas, Nuclear, Hydraulics, Thermal, Renewables, etc.

Any connection to the grid will draw this energy mix.

Showing and logging the energy usage of chain infraestructure will be beneficial in order to assess our footprint as a validator. We'll experiment on trying to complete energy usage with more green sources that we can clearly verify.

Our machines in Spain (Wind01, Wind02) and Finland (Wind08, Wind09) run 100% renewable as per contract. Utilities: Repsol (Spain), Oomi Oy (Finland, Hetzner as well).

Three of our machines that live in a datacenter (Colocation): Wind10, Wind13, Wind14, work with 100% renewable according to the host (Hetzner).
The german machines Wind04, Wind05 will run 100% renewable by 01.05.22. Utility: Swb.
