The Wind DAO

The WIND DAO is an experiment made the night of the 03.02.2022, where we minted the $WIND token and a DAO in order to experiment with governance. The $WIND token was airdropped to $JUNO stakers at validator "windpowerstake", and this was done in this way in order to make it easy.

Tokenomics

50%: 8214 $WIND in the DAO Treasury

50%: 8214 %WIND divided between 74 initial delegators (03.02.2022)

As the initial distribution was our first attempt, we are going to ask all initial delegators to experiment with staking tokens in the DAO. In the case we manage to LP the $WIND tokens, there is little control we can exert, as we own just 2 of the initial 74 delegators (1.3%)

In any case we recognize that the initial distribution is not optimal and we want to include newcomers, huahua delegators, nomic delegators and neighbouring participants on active, small validators. Participation means presence, voting, development of tools, helping and experimenting as well.
