3. Increase minimum commission rate to 5% 2021-10-07 NO
(before joining as validator)		 Our reason was that we were thinking that juno would not immediately have the commission needs that Osmosis have. Watching at the Hub, it made sense to us to keep a 4% commission as a minimum threshold. This would allow also the highest Top10 players (which are are actually well covered) to accept delegations if somebody really liked their job. We know centralization can occur, but we don't want to limit somebody to stake with their favorite validator just because it's Top10. (e.g.: SG1 was at 3.75%) YES However in the aftermath, we are one of the zones with most of the IBC traffic, and also some serious hardware requirements to be holding. Therefore in order to avoid running at losses this prop was important.
