Welcome to our website. Windpowerstake is a validation service operating on Juno, Evmos, Chihuahua, Nomic, Cerberus, Teritori, Rebus.

We secure approximately 638k$.

We take the world with irony (doge), and our stake with all the care. We work all with cold redundancies and we trust in monitoring, checking, being present and shifts. All processes priorize safety and robustness of the entire system over minor stats. By staking with us, not only you are helping a small team to decentralize the staking spectrum, but also you are contributing to the chain integrity itself (since we'll do).

We own our machines and we have taken the experimental approach of measuring their power consumption :D

Feel free to read about our validation approach, little projects and objectives using the menu on the left.
